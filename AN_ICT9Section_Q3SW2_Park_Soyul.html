The SpongeBob SquarePants Movie(2004)

Storyline

SpongeBob SquarePants takes leave from the town of Bikini Bottom in order to track down King Neptune's stolen crown. After King Neptune's crown is stolen, SpongeBob and Patrick go on a quest in 6 days to retrieve his crown. On the way SpongeBob and Patrick defeat many evildoers using their brains and bronzes. While this is happening someone is taking over Bikini Bottom and SpongeBob and Patrick must defeat this mastermind.
